About Me

Hi! My name is Connor Cote. I have three older siblings, four nephews (soon to be one niece), many many cousins and spend the majority of my time focused on family, friends and work. I am in this class primarily to help my current career in the technology consulting field. I have a business background and want to learn more about web development and coding in particular. Pictured to the left is my older brother (Kevin), myself and my dad. This picture was taken at a football game - crazy to think about attending a large sporting event now! My story really starts in Atlanta where I was born and raised. I attended Saint Jude elementary school before attending Marist School starting in 7th grade. After Marist, I went north to attend Boston College (a school where I felt at home since all three of my siblings, my parents and my in-laws also attended). At Boston College, I majored in Finance and Accounting with a French language minor. I also had the opportunity to spend some time abroad during my junior year spending first semester in Paris, France (I was there in during the 2015 bombings and terrorist attack and am happy to discuss it if you are interested). I spent second semester in Hong Kong and traveled throughout Southeast Asia.

After graduating from Boston College, I moved to New York City and started my career in Investment Banking at Bank of America Merrill Lynch. After 4,000+ hours, I realized that banking was not the career path I wanted and switched directions into consulting. Now I work as an Operations Associate for Francisco Partners Consulting which is the consulting arm of the private equity comapny, Francisco Partners. My goal for taking this class is to develop the ability to work closely with the product teams at the companies we invest in.
